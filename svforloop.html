üçï Example 1: Distributing Pizzas (Simple For Loop)

Say you have 4 pizzas and you want to deliver them one by one.

  initial begin
    for (int pizza = 1; pizza <= 4; pizza++) begin
      $display("Delivering pizza #%0d", pizza);
    end
  end
endmodule

      $display("Roll #%0d is %s", roll, present[roll]);
    end
  end
endmodule

üßæ Output:

üé≤ Example 4: Rolling Two Dice (Multiple Initializations)

Roll two dice together (i and j) until both reach 6.

  initial begin
    for (int i = 1, j = 6; i <= 6 && j >= 1; i++, j--) begin
      $display("Die A: %0d, Die B: %0d", i, j);
    end
  end
endmodule

üßæ Output:

Die A: 2, Die B: 5
...
Die A: 6, Die B: 1
